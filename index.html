Ningyu Zhang (张宁豫)


Hi! I am Ningyu Zhang. I am an senior artificial intelligence researcher at Zhejiang Lab . I got my Ph.D in College of Computer Science in Zhejiang University, with the supervision of Prof. Huajun Chen. And I obtained my bachelor degree from the College of Software Engineering of Zhejiang University in July 2012.

My research interests mainly lie in Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing, Knowledge Graph and Cross-media Intelligence.
